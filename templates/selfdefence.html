Travel Safety Tips for Women

Daily commuting or long-distance journeys can be safer if precautions are taken.

  • Wait in well-lit and crowded areas.
  • Use verified cab services & share ride details.
  • Sit near families or other women in transport.
  • Keep money and ID in secure, separate places.

Building Confidence & Self-Defense

Confidence and basic self-defense knowledge make women less vulnerable to threats.

  • Take practical self-defense classes.
  • Practice using a strong, assertive voice.
  • Learn simple escape techniques.
  • Stay active to boost reflexes & strength.
